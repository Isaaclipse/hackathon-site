NS Hackathon 2017

November 10 - 11Lehman College - Bronx, New York2017

What's this about?

The chapters of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) and the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) at Lehman College are teaming up this year to bring you our 4th annual hackathon! Our focus is on highlighting, supporting, and encouraging student talent from underrepresented and diverse backgrounds and provide a platform for you to build amazing things for your community.

Register now to join our hackathon in November and receive more updates along the way!

What if I don’t know how to code?

Prior coding knowledge is not necessary or required! All you need is a positive attitude, a willingness to learn, and a passion for creating and bringing ideas to life. We will have mentors and volunteers available to help you out if you get stuck or need help throughout the day. Remember, all skills are valuable!

Do I need to have a team to participate? How many people can I have on my team if I do have one?

Registering with a team is not necessary but we do encourage you to form a team before the event. If you do not have a team, do not worry! We’ll have a team networking session at the beginning of the event. We ask that teams be 4 to 6 members.

How much does it cost?

Our hackathon is free for all participants! We got you on WiFi, meals, refreshments, workspace, and more for the day of the event.

What do I need to bring?

Bring a valid form of ID (student ID is included), laptop and charger, as well as anything else to make yourself comfortable. We will provide food, music, snacks, refreshments so don't worry on that front.

Do I have to stay overnight?

Yes. All participants on a team must stay for the entirety of the event. That's the most exciting part!

Are there travel reimbursemenets?

Unfortunately, we are unable to offer any travel reimbursements at this time. :(

Can I win anything?

Oh yeah! There will be prizes to be won, fun to be had, things to be learned! Come prepared to give it your all, enjoy yourself, and possibly win a prize along the way.

Will there be photographing? What will my picture be used for?

Photos may be used on the Lehman College Website, the NS Lehman website, NS Lehman social media accounts, in future hackathon promotional material, and by our corporate and media sponsors. Your photos from the hackathon may not otherwise be used by any unauthorized third-party outside of Lehman College, our sponsors, or the nslehman.com website without your express permission. If you would like to know more about our photo policy, please send us an e-mail at ns.lehman@lc.cuny.edu.

I have a different question or comment. How do I contact you?

Don't be shy! Send any and all questions you have our way by sending an email to ns.lehman@lc.cuny.edu
